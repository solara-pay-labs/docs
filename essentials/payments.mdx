## Overview  

The [**Payments**](https://solarapay.fun/dashboard/payments) page in Solara Pay provides a record of all **payroll disbursements** made through the platform. It helps you keep track of every transaction, showing key details such as the payroll group, recipient, amount, and transaction signature.  

## What You Can See  

The **Payments** table includes the following details:  

- **Payroll Group** – The group associated with the disbursement.  
- **Recipient** – The wallet address or name of the person receiving the payment.  
- **Amount** – The total amount sent.  
- **Currency** – The digital asset used for the payment (e.g., USDC).  
- **Network** – The blockchain network through which the payment was processed (e.g., Solana).  
- **Signature** – The transaction hash for verification on the blockchain.  
- **Date** – The exact timestamp of when the payment was made.  

## Why It Matters  

The **Payments** page ensures you have full visibility into your payroll transactions, making it easy to review past disbursements, verify payments on the blockchain, and keep records of all activity.
