### **Sign Up for Solara Pay**

To start using Solara Pay, you need to create an account.

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion icon="user-plus" title="Create an account">
    1. Visit [Solara Pay](https://solarapay.fun/signup) and click **Sign Up**.  
    2. Enter your **email**, **business name**, and **password**.  
    3. Verify your email address via the confirmation link sent to your inbox.  
    4. Set up your Solana wallet for transactions.  
    5. Once verified, you can access your dashboard.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

### **Sign In to Your Account**

If you already have an account, follow these steps to log in.

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion icon="dashboard" title="Access your dashboard">
    1. Visit [Solara Pay](https://solarapay.fun/login) and click **Sign In**.  
    2. Enter your registered email and password.  
    3. Click **Log In** to access your dashboard.  
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
